Who we are

Powering discovery through computational science

We partner with researchers across PBRC to design, execute, and interpret complex multi-omics studies — from experimental design to final publication.

The Bioinformatics and Computational Biology Core supports investigators at the Pennington Biomedical Research Center by providing expert data science, statistical analysis, and bioinformatics services tailored to nutrition and metabolic disease research.

Our team works across the full data lifecycle: study design, sequencing data processing, multi-omics integration, machine learning modeling, and visualization — with a commitment to reproducible, publication-ready science.

We maintain a curated directory of the most relevant public databases in single-cell sequencing, epigenomics, proteomics, metabolomics, and more — accessible via this site — to support researchers in finding the right data resources for their projects.

What we offer

  • NGS data analysis: RNA-Seq, ChIP-Seq, ATAC-Seq, DNA methylation
  • Multi-omics integration across genomics, proteomics, metabolomics
  • Single-cell analysis: scRNA-Seq, scATAC-Seq
  • Machine learning and AI-based predictive modeling
  • High-performance computing (HPC) pipelines
  • Grant and manuscript writing support
  • Ongoing education, training and collaborative research
